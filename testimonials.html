How we approach feedback

After an event, families and partners can comment on the areas below. Verified testimonials can be added here later with clear attribution and consent.

1

Planning and communication

We ask whether the schedule, venue requirements, package details, and next steps were explained clearly before the celebration.

2

Children’s experience

We review whether the activities were age-appropriate, engaging, inclusive, and suitable for the group and venue.

3

On-site coordination

We invite practical feedback about arrival, setup, activity flow, adaptability, and the overall organization of the event.
