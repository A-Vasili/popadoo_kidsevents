About Popadoo

Popadoo Kids Events creates playful, well-organized celebrations for children and families. We help shape the theme, activities, timing, and practical details so hosts can enjoy the day with their guests.

Your venue, our team

We bring the celebration to you

Popadoo does not operate from a fixed party location. Our organizers travel to the home, school, event hall, garden, or other client-specified venue listed in the booking request. We confirm access, travel arrangements, setup needs, and availability before the event.

A flexible setup for every celebration

  • You choose the venue and share the full address.
  • We adapt the plan to the available indoor or outdoor space.
  • Travel requirements are agreed before a booking is confirmed.
